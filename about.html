About the Project

HydroTracker is a digital water monitoring solution developed under the Tamil Nadu Governmentâ€™s Smart Village Mission. It uses Internet of Things (IoT) technology to help monitor water usage, water levels, and quality in real-time across households and public water storage tanks.

The primary goal of this system is to reduce water wastage, promote transparency, and ensure equitable water distribution throughout Tamil Nadu.

Key Features

  • Real-time water usage tracking
  • Tank level monitoring
  • Water quality testing
  • Leak detection
  • User dashboard and government monitoring

Supported by

  • Department of Water Resources, Tamil Nadu
  • TNeGA - Tamil Nadu e-Governance Agency
  • Smart Village Mission Program
